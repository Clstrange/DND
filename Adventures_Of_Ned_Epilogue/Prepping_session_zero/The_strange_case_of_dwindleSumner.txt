---Dwindle Unique Ability---

Each morning roll a Wisdom check DC-15

Fail:
- Dwindle's personality changes to be more aloof
- Dwindle's senses are mixed with all of the surrounding wild life, struggling to tell what thoughts and feelings are his own
- Acts more on instinct than on reason
- Is susceptable to outside influence
- Pain in numbed as he stops resisting the power of Nature Spirit inside of him
- Disadvantage on Wisdom checks (When in nature dense areas)
- Disadvantage on Charisma checks

Succeed:
- Feels constant pain
- Is perfectly lucid
- Can seperate his senses from the nature around him and use it to his advantage
- Advantage on Nature checks
- Advantage on Perception checks in nature dense areas
