			---Magic Licenses---
Description:
	The human capital Alankar is very stingent on the obtaining and trading
	of magical items. Therefore every person must obtain a license before 
	being allowed to legally own or trade magical items. Magic items are
	categorized on the basis of there power and there is a seperate license
	for each category. Fines will be given if personage is caught trading,
	possessing, or using a magical item without the proper license. All
	items above grade D must be registered to the owner through the guild
	of commerce.


---Grade: D---

Description: Any magical item that is unable to directly cause harm to 
	     and individual. The item must be classified as "harmless."
Requirements: No criminal record
Fee: 5 gold
Fine: 10 gold
